What Is Fishing?

Fishing is a popular outdoor activity that involves catching fish for sport or food. It can be done in freshwater lakes, rivers, or saltwater oceans.

Common Fishing Techniques

Common techniques include fly fishing, baitcasting, and trolling. Each method requires different equipment and skills.

Why People Enjoy Fishing

  • Fishing is relaxing
  • It helps you enjoy nature
  • You can fish with friends or family

Basic Steps to Start Fishing

  1. Choose a fishing spot
  2. Select the right bait
  3. Cast your line
  4. Wait for a bite
  5. Reel in the fish
